We're hiring!

At NuMat, we take pride in our company culture. We actively cultivate a positive and exciting work environment; in doing so, we look for much more than technical competency in new team members. We look for people with a specific set of traits, exclusively hiring those who would thrive in a fast-paced startup environment.

The rest of this page breaks down the exact traits and skills required to be successful at NuMat. If you fit the bill and are interested in joining, we will work with you to create a role that suits your needs. We are open to internships, full-time positions, and everything in between.

Our Values

As a team, we maintain a core set of values that we believe are key in the success of NuMat as a whole. These values are listed below. On the right are mentalities, traits, and personalities that correspond to our overarching values. If you find yourself relating to this list, then NuMat may be the place for you!

We Dream Big.

We solve the world’s toughest problems. We take risks, push boundaries, constantly question and reject the status quo to do so.

  • See the big picture
  • Challenge the status quo
  • Are rarely satisfied
  • Set ambitious, aggressive targets
  • Actively obsolete other technology

We Grow Together.

We cultivate teamwork to help each other grow. We constantly seek, give and receive feedback. We embrace our development and work hard to improve as individuals and as a team.

  • Learn things outside of their core competency
  • Are aware of each other's development needs
  • Push themselves, are competitive
  • Know their strengths, and actively work to get even better
  • Own up to responsibility of giving constant feedback
  • Are not defensive, but rather open and appreciate feedback

We are Hungry.

We’re here to win. We will work harder, smarter and faster than anyone to do so. We never wait for tomorrow when we can get it done today. Speed is in our DNA.

  • Simplify processes and limit bureaucracies
  • Are comfortable working startup hours, not 9-5's
  • Are results focused
  • Demand a lot from themselves
  • Don't wait for tomorrow
  • Know what is really required, stripping away extraneous tasks

We are Scrappy.

We get it done with less – ahead of schedule, under budget, and always creatively. We wear multiple hats, move rapidly, are flexible and quickly adapt to changes, never looking back.

  • Think like an investor
  • Constantly challenge our priorities; change quickly
  • Aren't married to their ideas
  • Adapt quickly to changes
  • Value their time, working efficiently

We Take Ownership.

We set aggressive targets and take personal ownership for successes and shortcomings. We take initiative and seek out increasing responsibilities.

  • Own up to shortcomings / take responsibility
  • Recognize when they need help
  • Are not ambiguous on objectives
  • Don’t wait; proactively identify issues and take on
  • Are accountable to other’s objectives too
  • Think like a boss

We Over-Communicate.

We proactively and continually communicate our ideas, progress, needs and concerns. We are respectful, honest, direct and don’t mince words. We are concise, focusing on the important.

  • Actively know what all team members are working on
  • Are responsive and always available
  • Are prepared
  • Communicate concisely and effectively
  • Do not wait when it is important

We have Fun.

Life is short. We celebrate the successes in our adventures. We have fun, laugh, and never take ourselves too seriously.

  • Don’t take themselves too seriously
  • Have a sense of humor
  • Take initiative in maintaining team positivity
  • Enjoy building new friendships

Technical Responsibilities

We work with novel nano-materials, borrowing skillsets from chemistry, materials science, engineering, and computer science to do so. Above all, we expect people to be able to learn quickly and effectively on the job, working toward becoming an opinionated team player in a wide range of projects. That being said, there are skill sets we prefer to see.


Synthetic Chemist

Our metal-organic frameworks often consist of organic ligands that are not readily available. Therefore, we value strong backgrounds in organic synthesis. Preferably, one would have experience working with crystalline materials and sorbents, understanding the techniques used in characterization in these fields.


Software Developer / Computational Theorist

We use a variety of computational techniques to model the performance of our materials without needing to perform costly experiments. We commonly use C, Python, and Javascript, and we are avid supporters of modern scientific libraries such as IPython and PiCloud.


Chemical Engineer

For every material created, we need to consider the benefits of economies of scale. Knowledge of transport, thermodyamics, kinetics, and controls required, experience with crystalline precipitation reactions a plus.


Business Development Specialist

One of the largest tasks at NuMat is new market exploration. A fundamental understanding of the concepts underlying market forces and value propositions, as well as comfort in analytics and research, is required.
