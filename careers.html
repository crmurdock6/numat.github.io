We're hiring!

At NuMat, we take pride in our company culture. We actively cultivate a positive and exciting work environment; in doing so, we look for much more than technical competency in new team members. We look for people with a specific set of traits, exclusively hiring those who would thrive in a fast-paced startup environment.

The rest of this page breaks down the exact traits and skills required to be successful at NuMat. If you fit the bill and are interested in joining, we will work with you to create a role that suits your needs. We are open to internships, full-time positions, and everything in between.

Our Values

As a team, we maintain a core set of values that we believe are key to the success of both NuMat and its members. We look for people who would thrive in a startup culture, ensuring that every new team member will get the most out of their experience.

Technical Responsibilities

We work with novel nano-materials, borrowing skillsets from chemistry, materials science, engineering, and computer science to do so. Above all, we expect people to be able to learn quickly and effectively on the job, working toward becoming an opinionated team player in a wide range of projects.


Excited?

If you feel like you would fit in at NuMat, then we would love to hear from you. Send us an email with all of your questions and concerns, and, if you're looking for a professional opportunity, please explain why you think you would be a great addition to our team!
