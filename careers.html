We're hiring!

At NuMat, we take pride in our company culture. We actively cultivate a positive and exciting work environment; in doing so, we look for much more than technical competency in new team members. We look for people with a specific set of traits, exclusively hiring those who would thrive in a fast-paced startup environment.

The rest of this page breaks down the exact traits and skills required to be successful at NuMat. If you fit the bill and are interested in joining, we will work with you to create a role that suits your needs. We are open to internships, full-time positions, and everything in between.

Our Values

As a team, we maintain a core set of values that we believe are key in the success of NuMat as a whole. These values are listed below. On the right are mentalities, traits, and personalities that correspond to our overarching values. If you find yourself relating to this list, then NuMat may be the place for you!

We Dream Big.

We solve the world’s toughest problems. We take risks, push boundaries, constantly question and reject the status quo to do so.

We Grow Together.

We cultivate teamwork to help each other grow. We constantly seek, give and receive feedback. We embrace our development and work hard to improve as individuals and as a team.

We are Hungry.

We’re here to win. We will work harder, smarter and faster than anyone to do so. We never wait for tomorrow when we can get it done today. Speed is in our DNA.

We are Scrappy.

We get it done with less – ahead of schedule, under budget, and always creatively. We wear multiple hats, move rapidly, are flexible and quickly adapt to changes, never looking back.

We Take Ownership.

We set aggressive targets and take personal ownership for successes and shortcomings. We take initiative and seek out increasing responsibilities.

We Over-Communicate.

We proactively and continually communicate our ideas, progress, needs and concerns. We are respectful, honest, direct and don’t mince words. We are concise, focusing on the important.

We have Fun.

Life is short. We celebrate the successes in our adventures. We have fun, laugh, and never take ourselves too seriously.

Technical Responsibilities

We work with novel nano-materials, borrowing skillsets from chemistry, materials science, engineering, and computer science to do so. Above all, we expect people to be able to learn quickly and effectively on the job, working toward becoming an opinionated team player in a wide range of projects.


Excited?

If you feel like you would fit in at NuMat, then we would love to hear from you. Send us an email with all of your questions and concerns, and, if you're looking for a professional opportunity, please explain why you think you would be a great addition to our team!
