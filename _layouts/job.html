About NuMat

NuMat is an award-winning technology company committed to enabling fundamental performance shifts in gas storage and separation. NuMat is focused on commercializing metal-organic frameworks (MOFs), a class of high-performing sorbents that can be tailor-designed at the atomic level for specific uses. NuMat's unique capabilities are based on key technological advancements in computational design, laboratory synthesis, and large-scale production of MOFs, which has enabled NuMat to rapidly prototype full-scale products with strong market interest. To date, NuMat has identified materials that show significant promise in the storage of natural gas, electronic gases, oxygen, and industrial gases, as well high-value gas separation and purification.
