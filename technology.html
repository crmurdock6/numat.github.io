A material for every application.

At the heart of our products are a class of highly customizable materials. From this starting point, we build complete packaged solutions for industrial processes and consumer needs.

The next generation of sorbents.

We create products that take advantage of the scientific cutting edge. We do so by tailor-designing nanoporous materials - specifically metal-organic frameworks - for high-value applications which critically depend on material performance.

While you may not notice them, sorbents are everywhere in today's world. Activated carbon is used to purify our air and water, and zeolites turn air into medical-grade gas. These sorbents all function by exploiting a fundamental physical property: gases like to stick to surfaces. By containing trillions of nano-sized pores, sorbents provide a lot of surface for gases.

Sorbents are often categorized by their internal surface area, as more surface is correlated with higher performance. Our materials currently hold the surface area world record; in fact, a single gram of one of our materials would be able to cover 1.5 American football fields when unfolded. This means that gas molecules have significantly more contact area, drastically increasing the amount of adsorbed gas.

An elegant solution to material design.

Metal-organic frameworks are not only higher performing than their competitors, but possess a unique property that simply cannot be emulated with current materials: atomically precise tunability. By adjusting the starting materials used, a near-infinite amount of metal-organic frameworks - all with unique and controllable properties - can be created.

In contrast to non-crystalline materials such as activated carbon, this means that we are able to design materials that fully utilize their internal surface area. We control pore size and pore volume with sub-angstrom accuracy, and are able to covalently and uniformly bond functional motifs into the framework.

In order to take full advantage of the combinatoric complexity of this class of materials, we use a suite of proprietary computational tools to quickly narrow down on top performers. Our computationally driven approach to material discovery allows us to quickly assess the performance of our materials in an application without performing a single experiment.

Safer, cleaner, cheaper, better systems.

Processes and products that use sorbents are fundamentally limited by the properties of the material. This limitation is always present, even though it manifests itself differently across applications. Whether it means high-energy compression, impure offgas streams, low battery life, or logistical complexity, the ramifications of material limitations are felt across all gas-centric applications.

Our engineering team starts with the best sorbents in the world. This provides unprecedented system-level design flexibility, allowing our solutions to perform better, last longer, save energy, and ultimately cost less.

From portable oxygen generation to hazardous gas handling, our packaged products provide real value to real applications. We have a proven track record of delivering full-scale products into multiple high-value markets, and are continuing the creation of new prototypes while scaling validated solutions.
